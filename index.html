Dog Image Generator

Describe your dream dog and watch it come to life!

How to use
  1. Select a Breed: Choose from hundreds of dog breeds in the dropdown menu
  2. Generate Random Image: Click "Get Random Dog" to fetch a random image of your selected breed
  3. Get Another: Use "Another One" to get more images of the same breed
  4. Save Favorites: Click "Save to Favorites" to keep dogs you love
  5. Manage Collection: Remove individual favorites or clear your entire collection at once

💾 Your favorites are saved locally in your browser and persist between visits.
